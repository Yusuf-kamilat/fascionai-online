The Problem

Fashion runs on forecasts. Most forecasts are guesses.

Trend cycles that used to take a season now take a week. Your planning team is still working from trailing 12-month averages, gut instinct, and a merchandising meeting that happens too late to matter.

The result is the oldest problem in the industry, wearing a new mask: you’re either sitting on inventory nobody wants, or missing the sell-through on the one style everyone’s asking for.

Facsion AI exists to close that gap — permanently, not with a one-time forecasting report, but with a system that gets smarter every week you run it.

FAQ

Questions merchandising teams usually ask.

Who is Facsion AI built for? +

Merchandising, planning, and buying teams at fashion brands, retailers, manufacturers, and wholesalers who need forecasting and inventory intelligence beyond what spreadsheets or legacy BI tools can deliver.

How long does implementation take? +

Most customers are fully connected and generating live forecasts within 4–8 weeks, depending on the number of systems being integrated and the complexity of historical data.

Can it integrate with our existing systems? +

Yes. Facsion AI is API-first and integrates with modern ERP, POS, inventory management, and e-commerce platforms, along with reporting tools like Power BI.

Does it support multiple brands or regions? +

Yes. Facsion AI supports multi-brand, multi-region, and multi-entity deployments with configurable roles and permissions per business unit.

Is our data used to train models for other customers? +

No. Each customer’s forecasting models are trained on and scoped to their own data. Nothing is shared or pooled across accounts.

Is Facsion AI a SaaS product? +

Yes — fully cloud-based, with enterprise deployment options available for organizations with dedicated infrastructure requirements.
